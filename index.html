Our Story

Founded in 2023, KicksElite began as a small sneaker boutique in Ranchi, a small city in Jharkhand. What started as a passion project between three sneakerheads has grown into one of the most trusted sources for rare and exclusive sneakers worldwide.

We personally authenticate every pair that comes through our doors, ensuring you only receive 100% genuine products. Our team of experts hand-selects each release, focusing on quality, exclusivity, and cultural significance.
